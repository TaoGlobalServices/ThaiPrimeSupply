Corporate Overview

Thai Prime Supply Co., Ltd is a specialized B2B export and sourcing company headquartered in Bangkok, Thailand.

We serve as the centralized procurement and logistics arm for international wholesale buyers. Our operations are structured to bridge the gap between regional agricultural producers and global import markets, maintaining strict adherence to product specifications, compliance documentation, and freight timelines.

Operational Transparency

Weekly Shipment Schedules

Freight dispatch is synchronized with established air and sea carrier routes to maintain consistent inventory cycles for our buyers.

Pre-Loading Inspection

Onsite physical QC ensures accurate grading, weight verification, and defect elimination prior to final carton sealing and palletization.

Visual Dispatch Proof

Clients receive comprehensive photo and video documentation of their specific cargo immediately prior to airport handover or container sealing.

Temperature Control Handling

Thermal tracking is utilized from blast-freezing and active pre-cooling facilities through to reefer loading and final port arrival.
